First, stand up from the couch, shake off those Dorito crumbs, and go to the door.

Open the door, squint your eyes to the light from the sun, and walk out.

Once you finally make it out of the basement, breathe in that fresh air. You'll instantly forget the stale smell of cottage cheese and body odor.
