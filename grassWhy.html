Reasons to Go Outside

Contrary to what you may think, spending every day eating Doritos and playing Fortnite is not a good way to spend your life.

Nature provides opportunities for exercise and physical activity.

Being outdoors can reduce stress and anxiety levels.

Outdoor activities promote social interaction and bonding with others.

Exploring the outdoors allows for new experiences and adventures.

Connecting with nature fosters a sense of appreciation and environmental awareness.
