Why Go Outside?

  • Contrary to what you may think, spending every day eating Doritos and playing Fortnite in your parents' basement is not a good way to spend your life.

    • Going outside provides opportunities for exercise and physical activity.

    Being outdoors can reduce stress and anxiety levels.

  • Experiencing nature can boost creativity and inspire new ideas.
  • Outdoor activities promote social interaction and bonding with others.
  • Exploring the outdoors allows for new experiences and adventures.
  • Connecting with nature fosters a sense of appreciation and environmental awareness.
